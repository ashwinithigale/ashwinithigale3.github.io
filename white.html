WHITENING SKIN BY USING LASER TREATMENT


         27 March, 2020                  Blog               0      



To get healthy skin there are many options starting from chemical peeling to high-end lasers. We have a much wide range of options for treating the skin safely and effectively, these treatments are 100 percent safe and effective by a qualified and experienced professional using ungraded equipment. There are many types of lasers treatments one of them is fractional Co2 laser.


It is a very effective tool in improving the texture and quality of the skin and creating a brighter, smoother and more youthful appearance. This laser passes through the deepest layer of the skin called the dermis, where the laser stimulates and regenerates cells to produce collagen and ensures a more natural appearance, blending with the surrounding skin. the end result is skin with more elasticity and improved texture and quality and a more youthful appearance.


The CO2 laser is the gold standard in skin resurfacing. This laser has a specific wavelength of ten thousand six hundred nano-metres. At this wavelength, the main target of laser is water which is one of the main components of the skin. The laser interacts with the water in the skin and does two things. Firstly it vaporises a fine layer of skin that resurfaces wrinkles, skin so that it becomes smoother and It also heats a bit of skin underneath. The structure of collagen is basically like a spring which is held apart by a sulphate bond so that when it reaches a certain temperature the bonds break and the spring recoils that are the collagen spring recoils so that the skin may get firming. This results in the permanent and the treatment are usually just done once. When the procedure is done the physician will give local anaesthesia into the skin which will give asleep to the patient. This will provide analgesia or pain relief after the procedure.
