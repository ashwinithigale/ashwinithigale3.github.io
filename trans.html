TREATMENT FOR ANDROGENETIC ALOPECIA USING HAIR TRANSPLANT



         23 March, 2020                  Blog               0      



Androgenetic Alopecia is more commonly known as pattern baldness. Patients will keep an area around the back and sides of their head which is not affected by the balding process this is known as the donor region. When hair is removed from the donor region and transplanted into an area of the thinning or baldness. The hair will keep the characteristics of the area from which it came this means the hair will remain unaffected by the balding process.


No special medications or shampoos are required to maintain the transplanted hair. A patient will be awake throughout the hair transplant and will feel no pain. A local anesthetic is used similar to that used for dentist patients will relax watch movies and listen to music during their procedure. Once hair removed from the donor region each hair graft is individually prepared for transplantation and at the same time, small incisions are made in the recipient areas where the new graft is to be transplanted. Fascino body care one of the best hair transplant clinics in Hyderabad providing the doctors with the highly qualified and best treatment.


Hair graft naturally contains a group of hairs that can be between one and four hairs each. On average there are two hairs in each hair graft over 4000 hair grafts can be transplanted in a single day procedure. An average hair transplant procedure for a patient with moderate hair loss involves the transplantation of approximately 2000 hair grafts. The transplanted hair is permanent and the design of every patient’s transplant must be well-thought-out to ensure that it looks natural for the rest of the patient’s life.


An appropriately executed hair transplant should include very careful planning with a huge group of hair transplant experts and medical attendants should just be carried out by a qualified consultant surgeon. The procedure is carried out during the course of a single day taking between six to ten hours. Most patients return to work within few days without anyone being aware a hair transplant procedure has been carried out.
