What are the causes for hair loss?



         19 March, 2021                  Blog               0      



Hair loss is also known as alopecia. Alopecia is the term that includes both scarring and non-scarring types of hair loss. Different causes for hair loss include changes in hormones, stress as well as genetics, and the use of different types of hair devices. One of the best ways to determine hair loss due to stress or hair loss due to aging and hormones. One of the best ways to be able to identify this is by determining where the hair loss is occurring. Hormone changes the type of alopecia occurs called androgenic alopecia which occurs as thinning of hair in the front region of males and females and the scalp will be seen more visibly. Hair loss may not always be permanent for someone, treatments like cosmetic solutions, transplantations, and medications will help the people to re-grow their hair. Re-growth of the hair depends on the type of alopecia that patient has. Non-scarring alopecia will take more time and hair grows very slowly. Hair growth cannot be seen on a day to day basis. You will get full re-growth of your hair back to your normal baseline in non-scarring alopecia.

Scarring alopecia replaces hair with the scar. If the treatment starts early, then prevention of hair loss will further be stopped but if once scars take the hair follicle then the hair will not grow back.
